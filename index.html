About

Find Out More About Us

Water link is an app powered, one stop shop for all water services and products for smart phones. It is a water distribution company that aims to provide safe and clean water for domestic use in a convenient, reliable and efficient model.

The solution to your water needs.

We are committed to innovation, integration and social responsibility and we believe that everyone deserves easy access to this essential resource.

  • Vision

    We aim to be a one stop shop for all water needs. The plan to provide safe and affordable water for consumption and other use through providing a technology enabled water distribution systems, bottling and more

  • Mission

    Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable, convenient and sustainable access to clean water for individuals and communities in need.
